Guest Rooms

We have two luxury bedrooms:- The Four Poster Room and The Blue Room:

The Four Poster Room

The four poster room has a comfortable king-size bed with luxury bed linen and siberian goosedown duvet. It has its own adjacent private bathroom with large separate shower with a selection of luxury toiletries made for us in Norfolk. The room is very comfortably furnished with a sofa, colour television with freeview, hairdryer and a tray with assorted teas, coffee and hot chocolate. Dressing gowns are provided for your comfort. From the window you look along the sea wall towards the sea with Holkham Woods and Wells Lifeboat Station in the distance.

The Blue Room

The Blue room has a comfortable king-size bed luxury bed linen and siberian goosedown duvet, hairdryer, colour television with freeview, drinks tray with assorted teas, coffee, chocolate etc. There is an ensuite shower room with a selection of luxury toiletries. Again the view from the window is towards Holkham Woods and the Lifeboat Station.
