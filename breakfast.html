Our Breakfast

Breakfast is served in the old Butchers Shop, our full English breakfast is made using only local bacon, sausage, free range organic eggs, tomatoes and mushrooms. Homemade bread, jams and marmalade, cereals, fruit juice and fruit compote are included.

Vegetarian diets fully catered for.
